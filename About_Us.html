About Us

Our focus is helping you find financial freedom from the complex accounting and tax needs. Crofts Accounting is an accounting firm in Bozeman and Belgrade that concenttrates on the needs of small to medium size business. We have been in the area for the past fifteen years. We focus on providing financial advice that lets us take the worry and stress out of your financial life. twenty five years of experience in public accounting and private industy working in the area of audit, tax planning and preperation and financial statement preparation. Working with small business and non profit organizations designing accounting systems, setting internal control policies and procedures, preparing grant reports, budgets, audit work papers, tax returns, computer installation and staff training.
